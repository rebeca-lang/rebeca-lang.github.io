About This Series

About the Series

Technology shapes how we work, interact, and make decisions, yet it is often designed within disciplinary silos. This series creates a space for dialogue across fields and for deeper reflection on responsibility, values, and societal impact.

🎓

Learn Across Disciplines

Hear from scholars working in computer science, philosophy, organizational research, and society.

💬

Join the Discussion

Each session combines a talk with open discussion designed to surface different perspectives.

🤝

Build Community

Meet others interested in human-centered approaches to systems, AI, and digital transformation.
